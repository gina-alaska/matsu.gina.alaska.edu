2011 MatSu LiDAR & Imagery

Public mirror of the Matanuska-Susitna Borough's 2011 LiDAR & Imagery Project. Members of the public can get a full copy (or subset) on a disk from the Mat-Su Borough LiDAR and Orthoimagery Data Ordering Portal.

Official MatSu Mirror: Imagery LiDAR Project Wide (not yet complete)
See the Project Summary.pdf and Project FAQ.pdf documents for details about the project.

GINA is working with the Alaska Energy Authority as part of the Susitna-Watana Hydroelectric project to make accessing the Matanuska-Susitna LiDAR and Imagery easier.

GINA Imagery and LiDAR services page WMS and custom mosaics/downloads

All the deliverables are public domain. See the Project Summary.pdf for details. Watch the project website for current status of the project deliverables.

  • ½ ft, ortho-rectified, 4-band imagery for a 270 sq/mi urbanized area - complete
  • 1 ft, ortho-rectified, 4-band imagery for the entire 3680 sq/mi area - complete
  • Point Cloud Data w/ 1m true nominal pulse spacing (nps) and 0.6m nps through overlap - in-progress
  • Automated “vegetation” classification; low (1-6ft), medium (6-15ft) and high (>15ft) - in-progress
  • Intensity Images - in-progress
  • 1 m first return digital surface model (DSM) (aka top of canopy) - in-progress
  • 1 m bare earth digital elevation model (DEM) w/ hydro-flattening - in-progress
  • 1 m first return hillshade - in-progress
  • 1m bare earth hillshade - in-progress
  • 2 ft contours - in-progress
  • Building footprints @ 97% accuracy - in-progress
  • Breaklines - in-progress

note: this is not a final or full mirror of the dataset yet. Watch the MatSu lidar-project-updates page for project updates and the log below for mirror updates.
